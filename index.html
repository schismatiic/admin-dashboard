Your proyects

IceNet

A community-driven communication network that connects penguins across distant icebergs using snow-powered signal towers. Designed to keep colonies united during migration season.

Linux on Ice

An adaptation of the Linux kernel optimized for extreme cold environments. Created by Tux and his team to ensure smooth operations for all Antarctic devices — from fish trackers to ice sensors.

FishOS

An open-source ecosystem for sustainable fishing. Penguins can track, manage, and share fish resources fairly. Built with transparency and teamwork — because every penguin deserves a meal.

Open Igloo

A collaborative housing initiative where penguins co-design and co-build modular igloos. Each block of ice is version-controlled, ensuring that no penguin is left out in the cold.

GlacierHub

A social platform for penguins to share updates, slides, and new fish recipes. Inspired by GitHub, but for icy ideas and flipper-powered innovation.

Arctic Beats

An open music lab producing ambient chillwave using natural iceberg sounds. Penguins remix wind, waves, and walrus vocals to create the ultimate polar soundtrack.
